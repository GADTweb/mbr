GLOBAL ACADEMIC DEVELOPMENT TRUST

Dedicated to promote, support, and develop high quality research skills, human resources, and academic activities in Global Halal Industry.

Background

GADT is a platform in the field of education to develop academic skills, professionals and share the knowledge in global halal industry. Different national and international organizations or universities are in collaborative relations with GADT for organizing conferences, seminars, workshops, and publishing journals. GADT is run by an excellent team of Management members who seek advices, suggestions, and recommendations from Board of directors, Advisors, Fellows, and associate fellows who come from both national and international institutes across the world.

MANAGEMENT TEAM

MISSION STATEMENT

1.    To serve knowledge seeking people with an excellent platform of knowledge sharing.
2.    To enhance future growth and development by improving academic and business performances, standards, and excellence embracing technology.
3.    Provide high quality services addressing the needs of academia-industry by unique conferences, seminars, workshops, and training programs. 

VISION

“Become a global iconic platform of knowledge sharing and skill development”
