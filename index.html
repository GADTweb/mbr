NEW ZEALAND GLOBAL ACADEMIC TRUST

Dedicated to promote, support, and develop high quality research skills, human resources, and academic activities globally.

Background

NZGAT is a platform in the field of education to develop academic skills, professionals and share the knowledge in global halal industry. Different national and international organizations or universities are in collaborative relations with NZGAT for organizing conferences, seminars, workshops, and publishing journals. NZGAT is run by an excellent team of Management members who seek advices, suggestions, and recommendations from Board of directors, Advisors, Fellows, and associate fellows who come from both national and international institutes across the world.

OPEN ACCESS POLICY

With the objective of ensuring the maximum spread of research innovations, and effective communication amongst scholars globally NZGAT also ensures a balanced requirments and rights of authors. The following two different open accesss types are reviewed on a regular basis based on the needs of the researchers in different academic fields we serve.  

GREEN OPEN ACCESS

It offers authors to make their papers openly available, free from charges and embargo periods. Once the article has been published, the author may submit or use the manuscript for any academic or non-commecial purpose.

LEARN MORE

GOLD OPEN ACCESS

It offers to publish in fully open access journals with an article processing charge based on the journal level. Authors may also choose to publish in ppartnership with different universities or associations where no charge is applied.
