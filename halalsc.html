INTERNATIONAL JOURNAL OF HALAL SCIENCE

The scopes of the International Journal of Halal Science and Technology (IJHS) include, but not limited to science, technology, innovation, creativity, economics, natural science, medical and health science, agriculture science. The journal is published in both printed and online versions. The ambition of IJHS is to become a recognized top tier journal, acclaimed for redirecting international halal science and technology studies for defining new directions.
